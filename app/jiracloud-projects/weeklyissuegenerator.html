Problem (real context)

Operational Jira tasks were created manually. This resulted in inconsistent issue structure, delayed creation, and limited traceability when reviewing historical execution.

Solution (step by step)
  • Scheduled Groovy job using ScriptRunner.
  • Dynamic calculation of week key and due date.
  • Idempotency check to prevent duplicate issues.
  • Standardized issue fields and ownership.
  • Execution logging for audit and debugging.
