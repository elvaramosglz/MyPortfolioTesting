Groovy / ScriptRunner

Weekly Issue Generator

Automated creation of standardized weekly Jira tasks using a scheduled Groovy job.

Problem
Weekly operational tasks were created manually, causing inconsistency and missed deadlines.
Solution
Implemented a scheduled ScriptRunner job to generate recurring issues with consistent fields and guardrails.
  • Reduced manual admin work and improved consistency.
  • Audit-friendly: predictable issue structure week over week.
  • Added logs for troubleshooting and traceability.

Permissions

Permission Schemes Audit & Cleanup

Automated extraction and comparison of permission schemes to identify duplicates/orphans after migration.

Problem
Manual comparisons were slow and error-prone across many projects and schemes.
Solution
Scripted auditing + reporting to standardize schemes and enable controlled rollout of a preferred model.
  • Faster scheme validation with clear evidence artifacts.
  • Reduced governance risk (inconsistent access).
  • Repeatable process for future migrations.
